Understanding Visit Keys

💡 Key Concept: The app identifies visits by MRN + Date
  • Same patient, different date = NEW record
  • Same patient, same date = UPDATE existing record
  • No duplicates created by accident ✅

Copy from Previous Visit

When entering the same patient on a new date:

Auto-fills ✅

  • Room, Hospital, Name, DOB, MRN
  • Treatment Plan, Supervising MD

Does NOT Copy ❌

  • Findings (fresh per visit)
  • Pending tests, Follow-ups

Selection Mode

Selection mode behavior: When you select one or more rows, a sticky Selection Mode bar appears and the app locks to Census and Table View until you clear the selection.
  • You can keep scrolling the table and keep selecting or unselecting rows.
  • The batch bar exposes Print Selected, Change Status, Archive, Delete, and Clear Selection.
  • Change Status opens a picker for the new workflow status and then requires a change note before saving.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Will the app work on my phone? ▼

Yes! The app is fully mobile-responsive and works on iOS and Android. Mobile-first design means it works great on phones and tablets. Your organizational login is required for security.

Why can't I switch tabs or views while rows are selected? ▼

That is expected. When one or more rows are selected, the app enters Selection Mode and locks you to Census and Table View so batch actions stay predictable. Clear the selection to move to other tabs or switch to Card View or By Hospital.

What happens to unsaved changes? ▼

Unsaved changes are lost if you close the browser. Always click Save before closing the modal or leaving the page. This is standard web app behavior.

Can I edit archived patients? ▼

Yes! Click the Archive tab, find the patient, open their record, make changes, and Save. You can also click Restore to move them back to the Census tab.

How long is patient data kept? ▼

According to your hospital's data retention policy. The Audit tab keeps access logs. Ask your IT administrator or compliance officer for specific retention schedules.

Can I access from home? ▼

Yes! The app is cloud-based and accessible from anywhere with internet. Your organizational SSO login provides secure access. No VPN required if your organization allows it.

How is my data encrypted? ▼

All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest in SharePoint. Access is audited and logged. Your organization controls all data within its M365 tenant.

What does "Copy from Previous Visit" do? ▼

It copies administrative fields (Room, Hospital, Name, DOB, MRN, Plan, MD) from the patient's last visit. It does NOT copy Findings, Pending, or Follow-ups (those are fresh per visit). Saves time on repeated entries!

Local Mode vs. Connected (M365)? ▼

Local Mode: App works in your browser without cloud sync. Fast, no setup, data stored locally. Perfect for testing.

Connected (M365): Data syncs to SharePoint Lists. Team collaboration, permanent storage, multi-user real-time features. Ask your IT admin to enable.

"Import New Only" vs "Import All"? ▼

Import New Only: Add new records only, skip any duplicates (MRN+Date match existing). Safe choice if unsure.

Import All: Add new records AND replace duplicates with file data. Use this when you have corrected/updated data to overwrite.

🔧 Troubleshooting & Support

🔴 Can't Log In

Symptoms

"Unauthorized" or "Access Denied" when signing in

Solutions

  1. Use your organizational email (firstname.lastname@hospital.org)
  2. Check Caps Lock
  3. Try incognito/private mode (clear cache)
  4. Try a different browser
  5. Ask IT admin to verify your role assignment in Entra ID

🟡 Shows "Offline" or "Local Mode"

Symptoms

Status shows Offline/Local Mode but you have internet

Solutions

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Open developer console (F12) for error messages
  3. Refresh the page (Ctrl+R or Cmd+R)
  4. Check if the API backend is running (ask IT admin)
  5. Try again in 30 seconds (polling may be catching up)

🔴 Can't Save Patient

Symptoms

Click Save but nothing happens or you see an error

Solutions

  1. Fill required fields: Room, Date
  2. Check for duplicate: Same MRN + date? Use "Copy from Previous Visit"
  3. Billing codes hidden? You may need billing permissions
  4. Check your internet connection

🟡 Data Not Syncing with Teammates

Symptoms

Another user made changes but you don't see them

Solutions

  1. Wait 15 seconds (app polls every 15 seconds)
  2. Click refresh button in status area
  3. Refresh the page (Ctrl+R)
  4. Close and reopen the patient record
  5. Verify both users are online and connected

🔴 App is Slow or Laggy

Symptoms

Fields lag when typing, slow to update

Solutions

  1. Close other tabs/apps to free up memory
  2. Clear browser cache (Settings → Clear browsing data)
  3. Try a different browser (Chrome vs. Safari vs. Edge)
  4. On mobile: close other apps and restart device
  5. Check your internet speed

🟡 Patient Still Shows After Archiving

Symptoms

Patient appears in both Census and Archive tabs

Solutions

  1. Refresh page (Ctrl+R)
  2. Wait 15 seconds for sync
  3. Close and reopen the app
  4. Try archiving again
